Madam Weatherberry's Academy of Magic

Welcome

Madam Weatherberry's Academy of Magic (Land of Stories Series) is a school, where Madam Weatherberry teaches her very few students to practice and control their magic. Magic has been outlawed in all four kingdoms over time, and the students must prove that magic is a good thing, which can help everyone in the long run.
